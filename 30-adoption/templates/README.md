# templates/ -- Transfer Kit for Your Environment

These templates help you apply the SHI Control OS methodology to your own AI workflow.

## Available Templates

| Template | Purpose | Use when... |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| [verification-skip-checklist.md](verification-skip-checklist.md) | Prevent verification skips | Your AI says "done" without evidence |
| [failure-naming-template.md](failure-naming-template.md) | Name and classify AI failures structurally | You observe a recurring AI failure and want to track it |
| [three-layer-starter.md](three-layer-starter.md) | Start three-layer role separation | You want to separate judgment/monitoring/execution |
| [delegation-spec-template.md](delegation-spec-template.md) | Specify delegation boundaries | You delegate tasks to sub-agents or helper AI |

## How to Use

1. Copy the template to your project
2. Fill in the blanks for your specific environment
3. Reference the template in your CLAUDE.md or system prompt
4. Observe whether the target failure mode is suppressed

These templates are designed to be self-contained. No other files from this repository are required.
